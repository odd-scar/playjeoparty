# Monetization Plan

Goal: fund ongoing updates without killing the fast party-game experience.

## Phase 1 (quick, low friction)
- Tip jar / support link
  - Add `Buy me a coffee` / Stripe payment link in lobby footer.
- Paid private theme packs
  - Sell category packs (sports-only night, SAT prep night, movie mega-pack).
- Sponsored room themes
  - Branded board skins for local events or creators.

## Phase 2 (core revenue)
- Pro host subscription (`$5-$12/mo`)
  - Larger rooms
  - Saved room presets
  - Match history + stats
  - Custom timer presets
  - Priority new category drops
- Team plans
  - Weekly trivia for schools, clubs, and companies with admin controls.

## Phase 3 (scale)
- Marketplace content
  - Let creators publish premium packs and split revenue.
- Seasonal events
  - Limited-time tournaments and prize pools with optional entry fees.

## Recommended pricing start
- Free: full casual play (keep growth high)
- Pro: `~$7.99/month`
- One-time category pack: `$2.99-$9.99`

## Product rules to protect retention
- Never gate base multiplayer behind paywall.
- Keep paid features as convenience, customization, and deeper stats.
- Avoid disruptive ad placement in the clue flow.
